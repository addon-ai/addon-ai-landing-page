## Usage Guidelines

### Recommended hierarchy

1. Blue Dark
2. Green
3. Teal
4. Blue Light

### Brand personality

- Professional
- Innovative
- Human-centered
- Technical but approachable

### Agent Design Rules

- Prefer blue-dark for typography and structure
- Use green for CTA and emphasis
- Avoid introducing non-approved colors
- Maintain accessible contrast
- Preserve visual consistency with ADDON AI identity
