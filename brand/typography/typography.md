# ADDON AI Typography System

## Typeface Roles

| Role | Font | Weight | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Roboto | Medium (500) | Logo and brand presence |
| Heading | Roboto | Medium (500) | Titles and section headers |
| Subheading | Noto Sans | Regular (400) | Supporting hierarchy and subtitles |
| Body | IBM Plex Sans | Regular (400) | Paragraphs and long-form reading |

---

## Typography Hierarchy

### Display
**Font:** Roboto Medium  
**Purpose:** Brand identity and hero presence

Used for:
- Logo support
- Large visual statements
- High-impact sections

---

### Heading

**Font:** Roboto Medium  
**Weight:** 500

Used for:
- Page titles
- Section headers
- Important UI hierarchy

Recommended characteristics:
- Strong contrast
- Clear structure
- Minimal decoration

---

### Subheading

**Font:** Noto Sans  
**Weight:** 400

Used for:
- Supporting titles
- Navigation labels
- Secondary hierarchy

Recommended characteristics:
- Clean
- Neutral
- Readable

---

### Body Text

**Font:** IBM Plex Sans Regular  
**Weight:** 400

Used for:
- Paragraphs
- Product descriptions
- Interface text
- Long reading blocks

Recommended characteristics:
- Comfortable reading rhythm
- High legibility
- Technical but human tone

---

## Suggested Scale

| Role | Size |
|---|---|
| Display | 48–72px |
| H1 | 40–48px |
| H2 | 32–40px |
| H3 | 24–32px |
| Subtitle | 18–24px |
| Body | 16–18px |
| Small UI | 14px |

---

## Agent Design Rules

- Preserve approved font hierarchy
- Do not replace primary typography
- Prefer Roboto Medium for titles
- Use IBM Plex Sans for readable body text
- Avoid decorative or script fonts
- Maintain visual clarity and accessibility

---

## Brand Tone Through Typography

ADDON AI typography should feel:

- Professional
- Contemporary
- Technological
- Human-centered
- Confident without visual noise
